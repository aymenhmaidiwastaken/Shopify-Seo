Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

ShopifySEO Pro is a Chrome browser extension for SEO analysis. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Summary: ShopifySEO Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays in your browser.

Data Collection

ShopifySEO Pro does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Browsing history or URLs you visit
  • Page content or analysis results
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Analytics or usage telemetry

Data Storage

ShopifySEO Pro stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • Analysis history: SEO scores and issue counts for pages you have analyzed, stored per domain. This helps you track improvements over time.
  • Rank tracking data: Keyword positions you have checked, stored locally to show rank changes.
  • Schedule settings: Your preferences for periodic re-analysis intervals.

All of this data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. It is never transmitted to any external server, third-party service, or any party whatsoever.

Data Transmission

ShopifySEO Pro makes network requests only in the following cases:

  • Site Crawler: When you explicitly initiate a site crawl, the extension fetches your site's sitemap.xml and page HTML to perform analysis.
  • Competitor Comparison: When you explicitly enter a competitor URL, the extension fetches that page's HTML for analysis.
  • Rank Checker: When you explicitly check a keyword ranking, the extension fetches Google search results to determine your position.

These requests are made directly from your browser. No data passes through any intermediary server. No results are sent anywhere.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To analyze the content of the page you are currently viewing.
  • storage: To save analysis history and settings locally in your browser.
  • sidePanel: To display the detailed analysis dashboard in Chrome's side panel.
  • scripting: To inject the SEO analysis script into web pages.
  • alarms: To support optional scheduled periodic re-analysis.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>): To analyze any website you visit and to fetch pages for the crawler, competitor comparison, and rank checker features.

Third-Party Services

ShopifySEO Pro does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services. There are no SDKs, pixels, or external scripts included in the extension.

Children's Privacy

ShopifySEO Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
